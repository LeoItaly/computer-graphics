Planar reflection project

Realistic reflections are integral to convincing computer graphics scenes, yet they are
nontrivial to achieve in a rasterization-based environment like WebGL. While ray tracing can
handle reflections of arbitrary complexity, planar reflections using conventional rasterization
require careful application of transformations, blending, and buffer techniques. In particular,
planar reflectors are commonly employed to simulate mirrors, still water surfaces, or polished
floors.
